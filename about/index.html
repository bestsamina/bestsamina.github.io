About me

Samina Fu is currently a master’s student in computer science at National Chiao Tung University (NCTU), Hsinchu, Taiwan. She has been a Network & Systems Engineer in NCTU Information Technology Service Center, where she developed the network operation platform, IT security platform development using Python and Django since 2014. She is interested in Network, Cloud Native Computing and the various open source. Now, she is a member of CDNJS & TGmeetup. Also, she contributed many open source projects, such as Kubespray. Her current research interests include Software-Defined Networking (SDN), data plane acceleration and Cloud Native Computing - Kubernetes. Now, she is an intern at Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI), Hsinchu, Taiwan. She wishes the job she applies include programming, not just operation.
